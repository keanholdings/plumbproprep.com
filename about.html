Our Story

Built by a Dad.
For Every Plumber.

PlumbPro started with a simple question: why isn't there a good plumbing exam prep app?

My son is a plumber's apprentice. When he started preparing for his Journeyman licensing exam, I went looking for a solid study app — something with real IPC questions, detailed explanations, and a way to track progress.

I couldn't find one.

There were flashcard apps that barely scratched the surface. Study guides that hadn't been updated in years. Nothing built specifically for the IPC plumbing exam with the depth and quality that someone's career depends on.

What started as a way to help my son has grown into something much bigger.

As I dug into the numbers, the need became clear. The plumbing industry is facing a critical shortage of licensed professionals. Over the next decade, tens of thousands of new plumbers will need to earn their licenses to replace retiring workers and meet growing demand. Every one of them has to pass an exam.

So I built PlumbPro Exam — not as a shortcut, but as a real study tool. Every question is based on the International Plumbing Code. Every answer includes a detailed explanation and a code reference. The mock exams simulate the real test conditions. The progress tracking tells you exactly where you're strong and where you need work.

Why It Matters

Plumbing isn't just a job — it's the infrastructure that keeps communities healthy and safe. Clean water, proper drainage, safe gas piping — all of it depends on skilled, licensed plumbers who know the code inside and out.

Every plumber who passes their exam with PlumbPro is one more professional making buildings safer and communities healthier. That's not a small thing.

What's Next

PlumbPro is just getting started. We're continuously adding questions, expanding to more states, and building new study tools based on user feedback. The goal is simple: be the best exam prep resource available for plumbing professionals — and help as many people as possible earn their license.

If you're studying for your plumbing exam, PlumbPro was built for you. And if you have feedback or ideas, I'd love to hear from you at support@plumbproprep.com.
